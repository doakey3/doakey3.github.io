Cushings Template

Moon face and plethora (redness on the skin)


Overnight Dexamethasone Suppression Test: Patient is given 1 mg dexamethasone between 11pm and midnight. Serum cortisol level is measured at 8-9am the next morning.

If a patient has an abnormal initial test for Cushings, they should be referred to an endocrinologist for further management.

Do not do a dexamethasone suppression test in a pregnant patient, rather do a 24 hour urinary free cortisol and a late night salivary cortisol test

References

  • Uptodate. Establishing the Diagnosis of Cushing's Syndrome
  • Atlas of Clinical Diagnosis. Second Edition. M. Afzal Mir. 2003.
