Wart Treatment Template

For benign lesions, a single freeze-thaw cycle is sufficient. Plantar warts have been shown to have faster resolution with 2 freeze-thaw cycles.

50% of warts resolve at 1 year.

Having a healthy immune system is important for preventing warts. 90% of renal transplant patients develop warts within 5 years.
