Diagnosis Guide Treatment Treatment Description

Left on Left Torsion

Axis side down. Flex hips until motion palpated at the lumbosacral junction. Monitor the ceiling-side sacral sulcus while the patient pushes feet toward the ceiling for 3-5 seconds against equal resistance. Repeat 3-5 times, engaging new barriers. Assess sacral symmetry.

Left on Right Torsion

Axis side down. Extend the lower leg until motion is felt at the lumbosacral junction. Flex the top leg and put foot behind bottom leg knee. Patient pushes flexed knee toward ceiling x 3-5 secs against resistance. Engage new barrier. Repeat 3-5 times. Assess sacral symmetry.

Right on Right Torsion

Left Unilateral Flexion

Place hypothenar eminence on the posterior ILA. Push anteriorly and superiorly, with greater force during deep inhalation. Abduct and internally rotate the lower extremity. Reassess sacroiliac motion.

Left Unilateral Extension

Place hypothenar eminence on the posterior sacral base. Push anteriorly and inferiorly, with greater force during exhalation. Abduct and internally rotate the lower extremity. Reassess sacroiliac motion.
