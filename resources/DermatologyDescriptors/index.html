Dermatology Descriptors

I know what it is, help me describe it.

PapuleRaised lesion < 1 cm
NoduleRaised lesion > 1 cm
PlaqueA raised flat-topped area, may be formed from the coalescence of papules
TumorA large solid lesion > 2 cm in size
MaculeFlat, non-palpable area < 1 cm
PatchFlat, non-palpable area > 1 cm
VesicleWell-circumscribed fluid-filled lesion < 1 cm
BullaWell-circumscribed fluid-filled lesion > 1 cm
ScaleFlaky surface. May be fine, thick, greasy, loose, or adherent.
DesquamationLoss of skin
SerpiginousSnakelike, wavy track
InduratedHardened
VerrucousWart-like.
GuttateDrop-like.
DermatomalOccuring in a dermatomal region, like herpes

Acne Multiple open/closed comedones with scattered erythematous inflammatory papules and/or pustules
Alopecia areata Non-scarring patches with exclamation point hairs
Angular cheilitis Erythema and scale at corners of the mouth
Actinic keratosis Erythematous hyperkeratotic non-indurated papules
Androgenetic alopecia Non-scarring alopecia in male-pattern hair loss distribution
Atopic dermatitis (acute) Erythematous patches with weeping, crusting
Atopic dermatitis (chronic) Patches of dry (erythematous) skin with scale
Atypical nevus Irregularly bordered/colored macule/papule
Basal cell carcinoma Pearly macule/papule/nodule with telangiectasia
Blue nevus Blue macule/papule, no atypical features
Bullous pemphigoid Tense bulla or eorsions
Chronic solar dermatitis Mottled pigmentation/wrinkles
Contact dermatitis (acute) Erythematous patches with vesicles, weeping, crusting
Contact dermatitis (chronic) Erythematous patches with scale, lichenification
Cyst Subcutaneous papule/nodule with central punctum, no induration
Dermatofibroma Firm papule with dimple sign, central white area with hyperpigmented periphery
Folliculitis Folliculocentric, erythematous papules or pustules
Granuloma annulare Erythematous annular patches
Herpes simplex Vesicles and crusting on an erythematous base
Herpes zoster Vesicles on an erythematous base in a dermatomal distribution
Impetigo Honey-colored crusting
Insect bite Erythematous papules with central punctum/crust
Intertrigo Erythematous patches
Lichen planus Purple, polygonal, planar, papule/plaques with fine net-like scale
Lichen simplex chronicus Lichenified patches/plaques
Lipoma Soft, subcutaneous papule/nodule/swelling without induration
Lupus Erythematous patches/plaques with scale
Molluscum contagiosum Small papules with central dell
Nevus Flesh/brown macule/papule with normal color and border
Nummular eczema Coin-shaped, erythematous patch with dry skin/scale
Onychomycosis Dyskeratotic nail with subungual debris
Onychoschizia Dryness/ragged nail cuticle, fine splitting of the nail plate
Pityriasis rubra pilaris Salmon-colored patches with fine scale with islands of sparing with diffuse palmar-plantar hyperkeratosis
Prurigo nodularis Excoriated papules
Psoriasis Erythematous papules/plaques with silvery (micaceous) scale
Rosacea Central facial erythema and telangiectasia (with erythematous inflammatory papules)
Sebaceous hyperplasia Yellow papule with central umbilication
Seborrheic dermatitis Erythema with (greasy) scaling in seborrheic distribution
Seborrheic keratosis Waxy brown/flesh/tan, stuck-on papule
Skin tag (acrochordon) flesh-colored/tan/brown pedunculated papule
Squamous cell carcinoma Erythematous, (indurated), scaly macule/papule/nodule
Stasis dermatitis Mottled pigmentation, erythema and dry skin with scale
Tinea Annular erythematous patches with scale (leading edge)
Tinea versicolor Hyper/hypopigmented patches with fine scale
Verruca Keratotic/verrucous papule with black dots and obliterated skin lines
Xerosis Dry patches of skin
