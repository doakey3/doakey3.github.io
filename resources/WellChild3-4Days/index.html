Well Child 3-4 Days Old Template

If not getting enough breastmilk, increase frequency of feeds to 10 times in 24 hrs and pump after breastfeeding, giving any extracted milk to the baby at the next feed.

If mother does not want to give vitamin D to baby (recommended), an alternative is for the mother to take 6000 IU daily of Vit D while breastfeeding.

Vegan mothers who don't use B12 supplements should give Vit B12 supplements to baby.

Weight loss of infants is most prominent in the first 3 days of life (while milk is coming in). Afterward, growth of at least 30 g per day is expected until 3 months of life.
