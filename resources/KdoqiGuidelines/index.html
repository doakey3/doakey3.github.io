General Principles for Monitoring for Adverse Effects When Initiacing ACE Inhibitors or ARBs

  1. Measure "baseline" blood pressure, GFR, and serum potassium
  2. Determine interval for follow-up measurements
  3. Counseling to reduce risk of hypotension
  4. Counseling to reduce risk of early GFR decrease
  5. Counseling to reduce risk of hyperkalemia
  6. Counseling for allergic reactions
  7. Counseling women of child-bearing potential about adverse effects on the fetus
