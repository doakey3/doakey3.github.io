Checking Home Blood Pressure

Use a cuff for the upper arm. Good cuffs: https://www.validatebp.org
Use a chair that supports a straight back.
Avoid loud noise.
Rest for 5 minutes beforehand.
Support the arm at the level of the heart.
Avoid caffeine, tobacco, or exercise 30 mins beforehand.
Keep legs uncrossed.
Use the right size cuff for your arm.
