Rate

  • Normal heart rate is between 60 and 100 bpm
  • For an irregular heartbeat, the approximate heart rate can be calculated by counting the number of R waves on a rhythm strip and multiplying by 10.
  • For regular heartbeats, the rate can be found by counting the number of large boxes between R to R waves.
    1 Box:300 bpm
    2 Boxes:150 bpm
    3 Boxes:100 bpm
    4 Boxes:75 bpm
    5 Boxes:60 bpm
    6 Boxes:50 bpm
    7 Boxes:43 bpm
