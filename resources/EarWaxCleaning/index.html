Ear Wax Cleaning

Ear Wax Cleaning Kits are found in most pharmacies.

No prescription needed.

Use the wax-softening agent twice daily for up to 4 days.

Do NOT use wax-softening if:

  • You have a current or recent ear infection or pain
  • You have a perforated eardrum.
  • You have drainage coming from the ear.
  • You've had ear surgery.
Use cotton balls to keep the softening agent from dripping out.
Spray warm water gently into the ear.

Dry ears after rinsing.

or

Place 1-2 drops of vinegar into the ear.
