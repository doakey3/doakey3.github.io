LDL Goals

For a patient with hx of stroke, target an LDL < 70. Add Ezetimibe if needed.

Lower LDL level by 50% in all patients with clinical ASCVD, those w/10-year ASCVD risk >= 20% or 40-75 yo w/diabetes

If LDL was ever > 190, use Ezetimibe if needed to get < 100 if max statin isn't enough.

Adults 40-75 wo T2DM & 10-year ASCVD risk > 7.5%, target a 30% reduction.

Recheck a lipid panel after 3 months to measure the reduction and determine if higher dose/change in medication is needed.

2018 Guideline on the Management of Blood Cholesterol
