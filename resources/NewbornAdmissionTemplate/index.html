Newborn Admission Template

Abnormal red reflex? Send to Ophthalmology

Isolated ear abnormalities (preauricular pits, cup ears) and dysmorphic features? Send for renal ultrasound.

Low set, posteriorly rotated, or microtic ears? Send for genetic testing.

Abnormal pulse oximetry at 24 hours of life or after? Send for diagnostic echocardiography.

Microcephaly (isolated asymmetric small head less than the 2nd percentil or 2 standard deviations below the mean for age and sex)? Consider genetic/nervous system syndrome.

Macrocephaly (greater than 98th percentile)? Send for brain imaging to evaluate for hydrocephalus or brain tumor.

Large fontanelle? Think Down syndrome, hypophosphatemia, trisomy, or congenital hypothyroidism.

Sacral dimple > 0.5cm in size, or > 2.5cm from anal verge or has hairy patches, discoloration, or hemangiomas, eval for spinal dysraphism w/ultrasound.

New born in breech position or Family hx of developmental hip dysplasia? Order hip ultrasound at 6 weeks or plain radiograph at 4 months.
