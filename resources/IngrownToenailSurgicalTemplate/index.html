Ingrown Toenail Surgical Template



Surgical Supplies

  • Nail splitter
  • Nail elevator
  • Straight hemostat x2
  • Iris Scissors
  • Alcohol swabs
  • 5-10 mL syringe, 27-30 gauge needle, filled with 1% lidocaine without epinephrine
  • Povidone-iodine sticks
  • Nonsterile gloves
  • 4 x 4 gauze
  • Fenestrated drape
  • Sterile rubber band
  • Bacitracin
  • Coban wrap
